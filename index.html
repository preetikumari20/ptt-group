About Us

Your trusted partner for IT and financial excellence in a digital world.

Who We Are

Founded in 2021 in New Delhi, India, PTT Group is a dynamic multi-domain firm specializing in IT solutions, cybersecurity, taxation, and accounting services. We bridge the gap between technology and finance to help businesses thrive in an increasingly digital landscape.

Our multidisciplinary approach combines deep technical expertise with financial acumen, enabling us to deliver holistic solutions that drive operational efficiency, ensure regulatory compliance, and foster sustainable growth for our clients.

Our Culture

Our team thrives on collaboration, continuous learning, and innovation. We foster an inclusive, supportive environment where diverse ideas flourish, empowering every member to contribute meaningfully and deliver exceptional value to clients.

We invest heavily in professional development, encouraging certifications, workshops, and knowledge-sharing sessions to keep our team at the forefront of industry trends.

What We Do

We provide end-to-end services ranging from custom software development, cloud migration, and AI integration to GST/ITR compliance, tax planning, bookkeeping, audits, and AI-driven business automation.

Serving startups, SMEs, and large enterprises across sectors like manufacturing, e-commerce, healthcare, education, and finance, we tailor every solution to unique business challenges and goals.

Our Commitment

We promise reliable, secure, and scalable solutions backed by expert guidance and proactive support. Our client-first philosophy ensures transparency, timely delivery, and measurable results.

With a strong emphasis on data security, ethical practices, and long-term partnerships, we help businesses navigate complex challenges and achieve sustained success in a rapidly evolving digital economy.

Company Profile

A journey of innovation, trust, and excellence since 2021

Our Foundation & Growth

Established in 2021 in New Delhi, PTT Group began as a passionate team determined to bridge advanced technology with expert financial services. In just a few years, we've grown exponentially, serving over 150 clients across diverse industries with high-impact, customized solutions.

Our rapid expansion is fueled by a commitment to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction, earning us recognition as a trusted partner for digital transformation and financial compliance.

Headquarters & National Presence

Headquartered in New Delhi with modern, state-of-the-art facilities, we maintain a strong pan-India presence through strategic partnerships and remote delivery capabilities.

Our centralized operations ensure seamless coordination, quick response times, and consistent service quality regardless of client location.

Innovation & Future Outlook

We have developed proprietary tools and frameworks in AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and automated compliance systems. As of 2025, PTT Group is actively expanding internationally while continuing to invest in emerging technologies like blockchain and advanced analytics.

Our R&D initiatives keep us ahead of industry trends, ensuring clients always benefit from cutting-edge, future-ready solutions.

Our Core Promise & Ethics

Business Services

Comprehensive solutions tailored for your success.

Custom Software Development

We design and develop tailor-made web applications, mobile apps, automation systems, and enterprise-grade platforms aligned precisely with your business objectives and operational needs.

Our end-to-end software development process covers requirement analysis, system architecture, UI/UX design, development, testing, deployment, and ongoing support. Using modern, industry-leading technologies such as React, Node.js, Python, REST APIs, and cloud-native architectures, we build secure, scalable, and high-performance solutions. Every application is optimized for speed, usability, data security, and future scalability, ensuring seamless performance across devices and long-term business growth.

Cybersecurity Solutions

Comprehensive cybersecurity services designed to protect your digital assets, data, and infrastructure from evolving cyber threats and vulnerabilities.

We offer vulnerability assessment and penetration testing (VAPT), network and application security audits, data protection strategies, and compliance support. Our cybersecurity experts implement advanced threat detection, risk mitigation frameworks, firewall and endpoint security, and incident response planning to safeguard your business against cyberattacks, data breaches, and operational disruptions.

Accounting & Taxation

Professional Chartered Accountant services covering accounting, taxation, compliance, audits, and payroll management for individuals, startups, SMEs, and corporate entities.

Our CA team provides end-to-end services including bookkeeping, GST registration & returns, income tax filing, TDS compliance, payroll processing, statutory and internal audits, and ROC filings. We ensure accurate financial reporting, timely statutory compliance, tax planning & optimization, and audit-ready documentation, helping businesses minimize tax risks, avoid penalties, and maintain financial transparency.

Cloud Migration

Secure, seamless, and efficient migration of applications, databases, and IT infrastructure to cloud platforms with minimal downtime and maximum reliability.

Our cloud experts manage the complete migration lifecycle including infrastructure assessment, cloud architecture design, data migration, security configuration, performance optimization, and cost management. We specialize in AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud, enabling businesses to achieve scalability, high availability, enhanced security, and reduced operational costs with continuous post-migration support.

AI Integration

Advanced AI-powered solutions that automate processes, improve decision-making, and enhance customer experience through intelligent systems.

We integrate artificial intelligence into your existing platforms through AI chatbots, workflow automation, machine learning models, recommendation systems, and predictive analytics. Our AI solutions help businesses automate repetitive tasks, analyze large datasets, predict trends, improve operational efficiency, and deliver personalized user experiences using data-driven intelligence.
